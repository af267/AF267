About Me

Hi, my name is James. I'm a high school student and an aspiring aerospace engineer and entrepreneur. I spend my free time designing rockets, ships, airplanes, stuff along those lines and cracking together random websites. I sometimes build rockets out of recycled materials in my basement (though I prefer designing bigger ones in Blender more), and if I do have the time, I might write something random in GarageBand.
